For over 25 years, The Pokémon Company International (TPCi) has been devoted to creating moments of joy, connection, and discovery for fans across the globe. Our mission is to delight through meaningful experiences that inspire friendly competition and shared journeys. Pokémon has grown into a cultural phenomenon that transcends age, borders, and language. We unite Trainers of all kinds through a world filled with incredible creatures and endless adventure. At TPCi, we believe in the power of play to bring people together and make the world a better, more connected place.

Our passion for Pokémon goes beyond games and characters. It drives a meaningful understanding of the impact we create for teammates, fans, players, and entire communities. We're deeply committed to supporting people both within and beyond our organization, through impactful philanthropy, inclusive benefits, and strong diversity, equity, and inclusion practices rooted in our culture. Through our Corporate Social Responsibility initiatives, we strive to bring the magic of Pokémon to life for children and families across the globe. Whether it's partnering with equity-driven organizations or creating joyful experiences for young Trainers, our mission is clear: to make a lasting, positive difference in the lives we touch.

Javin Mayawin, Erika Gualberto, Philip Malayao, and Aiko Tabako, members of the FUntrix, are four student-developers from FEU Tech, combining technical expertise with creative energy to support the world of Pokémon GO. Fueled by a shared love for innovation and gameplay, this dynamic team is committed to enhancing user experience, optimizing community engagement, and contributing fresh ideas to the evolving Pokémon GO universe. Whether it's through smart design, intuitive features, or real-world integrations, their mission is to help Trainers everywhere discover new adventures and build meaningful connections one tap at a time.
